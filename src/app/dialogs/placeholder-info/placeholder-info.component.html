Formatting Admission Letters

Format the admission letter and insert placeholders into specific places. Example of a placeholder is the full name of the student. The system will replace the placeholder with the actual student name when the online form is filled. Put the placeholder in-between two curly braces. E.g. {name}.

Supported Placeholders
Attribute Placeholder Usage
Student name name {name}
Admission number adminNumber {adminNumber}
Programme offered programOffered {programOffered}
Residential status residentialStatus {residentialStatus}
House allocated houseAllocated {houseAllocated}
Sample Letter Format

Dear {name},

Admission number: {adminNumber}

ADMISSION NOTICE, 2024/2025 ACADEMIC YEAR

I am pleased to inform you that you have been offered admission to SHS 1 as a {residentialStatus} student to pursue a 3-year course in {programOffered} as a result of your success in the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

You are allocated to {houseAllocated} and you are to report to school no later than 7TH NOVEMBER, 2024.
